## Project Pitch – SK Nail Studio

## User Persona
This app is for people who like nail art and want new design ideas. It also helps nail artists show their designs and work to others.

## Problem
People often do not know which nail design to choose. Nail artists also need an easy way to show their designs to clients.

## Solution
This app helps users explore different nail designs and pick what they like. It also helps nail artists easily share their work with others.
